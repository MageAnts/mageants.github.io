Magento 2 Limit Quantity For Grouped Products

Using Magento 2 Limit Quantity For Grouped Products Extension helps store owners set the minimum and maximum limit of product groups to buy.

Magento 2 limit quantity grouped by product Plugin is the best way store owners can keep a hold on the limits of the products. If customers add the product's number less than the minimum and maximum quality, then a message will pop up by restricting the additional products.

Magento 2 limit quantity grouped by product Module helps in driving more sales and increase the profit.

Key Features:

1. Magento 2 limit quantity grouped by product Module can be easily enabled and disabled.

2. The minimum order quantity is described in the shopping cart.

3. An alert message will pop up for the requirement of minimum order.

4. Choosing the wrong quantity will restrict the additional products.

5. User-friendly and easy to use.
