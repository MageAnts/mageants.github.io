Magento 2 Advanced Product Reviews

Using Magento 2 Advanced product reviews extension, a store owner offers customers to share their experience with store products or overall shopping experience. This will increase store visibility using those Magento 2 advanced reviews to display on any other sites.

Customers can choose products even as per rating views and share their thoughts using the like or dislike button or passing a message about their experience.

Key Features:

1. Easily know about user experience.

2. Easy for sending email or notification using different methods to customers.

3. Automatic review sorting order.

4. Display the review's section with all necessary information to share opinions.

5. Engage customers and motivate them to purchase faster.

6. Help customer to understand products through previous rated reviews.
