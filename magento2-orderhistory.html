Magento 2 Order History

Magento 2 Order History extension allows the store owner to have a look at the customer's previous orders and their reviews.

By using Magento 2 order history Plugin, the website owner gets help in looking at the order details. They can also edit the product pages and review the order history of customers. It also helps the website owners to edit the products more conveniently.

Key Features:

1. Customer products and orders can be viewed easily.

2. It is compatible with delete orders, order attributes, and order Grid.

3. Customers purchased items are listed and orders can be easily exported.

4. Helps in upgrading the order tab of the customer's detail page.

5. Sort, filter, show or hide the data and details of the order.
