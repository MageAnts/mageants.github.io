Magento 2 Out of Stock Notification

Magento 2 Out of Stock Notification extension can notify those customers who are interested in some products and they are out of stock, when products are available in the store and also this tool helps in sending a notification to those customers who subscribed about a particular product, now back in the store. And also inform the store owner about the list of customers to whom to send notifications about product availability.

Key Features:

1. Easily inform the customer about product buying.

2. Send bulk notification to users at the same time.

3. Manage the list of subscriber easily.

4. Customized notification with the name of products that are now available.

5. Send whole details with the latest offer to customers anytime.

6. Magento 2 low stock notification also send to store owner about their product availability count.
