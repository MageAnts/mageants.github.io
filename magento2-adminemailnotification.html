Magento 2 Admin Email Notification

Magento 2 Admin Email Notifications is one of the must-have extensions for merchants as it keeps them informed about all the important updates and activities performed on the website including low stock reminders, customer orders, customer wishlist, etc. via email notifications.

Using this amazing Magento 2 Admin Email Notifications extension, the admin will be notified and updated about all the significant events of their store, that too without checking your admin panel manually at regular intervals.

Key Features:

1. Send Emails for every important event that occurs on the website.

2. Send low stock email alerts to the admin .

3. Stay updated on new customer registration .

4. Email Notification about customer subscription to newsletters.

5. Customize and assign different email templates .

Using this extension, admins can provide prompt services to customers for better customer experience and satisfaction by spending more time and effort running their business smoothly.
