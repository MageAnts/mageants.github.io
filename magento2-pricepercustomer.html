Magento 2 Price Per Customer

Using Magento 2 Price Per Customer Offer personalized prices for each customer group by creating customer-tailored deals using Magento 2 Price Per Customer extension.

Custom Pricing is an additional feature for Magento 2 to reward your most loyal customers with special individualized prices. Now adjust tier price for customer group and product.

Key Features:

1. Create customer-tailored deals using personalized prices.

2. Option to offer personalized prices to specif customer group.

3. Updated special prices can be displayed in various places.

4. Individualized offers and exclusive prices to influence customers and motivate them to purchase.

5. Prices import/export via CSV file for fast prices management.

Optimize the price and reward your loyal customers to enhance customer satisfaction and increase engagement.
