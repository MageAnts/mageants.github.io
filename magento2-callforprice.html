Magento 2 Call for Price

Magento 2 calls for price extension mostly used by eCommerce store owners to set product price visibility as they want. Store owner redirects the product image to the specific page that shows the product price using "Call for Price" or any call to action button or even hide and show after adding to the cart.

Store owners can enable option calls for prices for specific customer groups and add Google ReCAPTCHA integration to avoid spam.

Key Features:

1. Magento 2 Ask for Price feature push customer to contact for asking product price.

2. Offer flexible prices according to customer groups.

3. Product customization price will add anywhere according to the store owner.

4. Show custom message instead of the product price.
