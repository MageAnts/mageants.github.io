Magento 2 Product Images by Customer

Magento 2 Product Images by Customer extension, a store owner can upload their product images to show a visual representation of products. The customer quickly knows how the product looks before selling them and after check, all images a customer buys without any confusion. Magento 2 customer product image extension tool helps in increasing the number of orders and conversion rate.

Store owners clear all product usability and show it in front of customers before placing orders, which solves all their queries about products and builds a strong customer relation.

Key Features:

1. Upload product images from the backend for a great customer experience.

2. Customers can register and upload product images to share their shopping experience with the store owner.

3. Allow a store owner to approve, reject, or delete any product images.

4. Enhance all the customer experience, your store credibility as well as your overall sales.

5. Improve product trust with real-time usage.
