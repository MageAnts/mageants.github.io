Magento 2 Free Gift

Magento 2 Free Gift extension helps store owners to offer customers free gift on purchasing particular products automatically. With this effective solution, buyers can select desired gifts from the popup and add the product to the cart.

Magento 2 Free Gift extension gives a gift offered to the store owner, which they give to their customers on purchase of some selected products to increase the sale.

As we all know that the specific offers, discounts, and gifts attract customers. Magento 2 Free Gift Extension offers multiple gift options to the customers to select according to their needs and requirements.

Key Features:

1. It helps in adding the gift items to the customer's cart automatically so that they can get the best offers.

2. The software keeps on updating the latest gift items in the main product.

3. The information on the gift items has been added to the main page.

4. Magenta 2 free gift plugin adds the gift banner on the product view page.

5. Admin is allowed to add conditions while offering gifts to the customer.

Magento 2 Free Gift Extension is an effective way to increase sales and make new customers. In addition, several incentives like discounts and gifts have proved beneficial in making new customers.
